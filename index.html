Meet Pico

Pico is a state-of-the-art mobile robot designed to seamlessly integrate into your indoor environment. Equipped with a versatile robotic arm and omnidirectional wheels, Pico is engineered for precision, adaptability, and intelligent operation.

Key Features & Abilities

Advanced Robotic Arm

6-axis high precision arm, lifting up to 2 kg.

Omnidirectional Mobility

Four Mecanum wheels for 360° movement.

AI-Powered Navigation

Cameras for mapping, obstacle avoidance, and autonomous planning.

Voice & Gesture Control

Intuitive interaction and task assignment.

Modular Design

Expandable for additional sensors or tools.

Object Recognition

Integrated computer vision to interact with objects.
